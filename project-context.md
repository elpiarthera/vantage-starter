## Identity

- **Product name:** VantageStarter
- **Description:** A production-ready SaaS boilerplate with a built-in AI agent team — real-time backend, generative UI, credit billing, and 8 specialist agents that know your codebase from day one.
- **Target user:** Solo founders, indie hackers, and small dev teams (1-5) who want to ship a SaaS fast without wiring Convex + Clerk + Polar + AI SDK from scratch.
- **Pain point:** Weeks of boilerplate setup — auth, billing, real-time backend, AI integration, design system — before writing a single line of product code. Plus AI coding agents that hallucinate your stack because they don't know it.
- **Differentiator:** Other boilerplates give you files. VantageStarter gives you a team — 8 AI agents with skills that know the codebase, an orchestrator that routes tasks, and hooks that enforce quality. Born agentic, not retrofitted.
